. Haberman dataset: 
      the data originates from a study conducted at the University of Chicago's Billings Hospital between 1958 adn 1970.
To analyze the survival rates of patients who underwent surgery for breast cancer. 1 stands for live 5+ years after the surgery; 2 stands for less than 5 years.
The dataset contains 306 instances (cases) of patients, with three main attributes: the age of the patient at the time of surgery,
the year of opreation (only tens and ones, such as 1986 only mark 86), and the number of positive axillary lymph nodes detected.
